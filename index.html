The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Renewable Energy

Bangladesh is committed to reducing its carbon footprint by investing in renewable energy sources such as solar and wind power.

Digital Transformation

The country is leveraging technology to enhance governance, improve public services, and empower citizens.

Robust Infrastructure

Bangladesh is investing in infrastructure projects to connect rural and urban areas, boost economic growth, and create jobs.
